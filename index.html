Pandemic Pedal #1
LPD K-1 Overdrive / Boost

First Pedal Build!

This is the Lawrence Petross Design K-1 Overdrive / Boost. I purchased this kit after seeing it built on Rhett Shull's YouTube channel. I enjoyed the experience oif building so much that I then bought 7 PCBs from Rullywow, a soldering station, and started sourcing parts from online retailers.

Pandemic Pedal #7
Rullywow TS8(oh)8
Ibanez™ TS808™ & TS9™ Clone

The tube screamer. Everyone needs to build at least one. I did my first mods on this. The Rullywow design has jumpers for fat, bright, and something??. Instrad of letting them be jumpers I installed switches on the side for the fat and bright, a switch on the front for something. And I made another switch to change between TS9 and TS808. The lLED changes from red to green depending on which tube screamer.
